Short history

The city of Chisinau, the capital of the Republic of Moldova, is located on seven hills, in the immediate vicinity of the Bâc river. The territory of the localities and the surroundings have been inhabited since the Paleolithic, among the most significant archaeological remains being those belonging to the Geto-Dacian cultures (IV-I centuries BC) and Sântana de Mureş-Cerneahov (III-IV centuries BC).

Localization

Chisinau is 448 km from Bucharest (Romania) and 471 km from Kiev (Ukraine), capitals of neighboring states.The city of Chisinau is located on seven hills located in the shape of a circle. On the highest hill is the Ciocana sector. The lowest point is the center of the city, the place where the city of Chisinau was founded, near the Bâc river, Albișoara.The area of the city of Chisinau is about 120 km.p., the area of the municipality is about 635 km.p.
